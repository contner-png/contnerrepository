Mechanism of Action

This high-power modality utilizes specific infrared wavelengths (typically 810nm-980nm) to penetrate deep tissue and trigger Photobiomodulation. Cytochrome c oxidase, a key enzyme in the cellular mitochondria, absorbs this light energy.
