Shockwave Therapy for Tendinopathy

Mechanism of Action

This technology utilizes acoustic pressure waves, not electricity, to deliver focused energy into damaged musculoskeletal tissue. Through Mechanotransduction, these shockwaves induce targeted micro-trauma at the cellular level. This stimulates the mandatory biological healing response, triggering the expression of Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor (VEGF), breaking down chronic calcific deposits, and restarting neo-vascularization (new blood flow) in chronic, avascular injuries.

Non-Surgical Healing for Chronic Conditions

Extracorporeal Shockwave Therapy (ESWT) is the gold standard for treating chronic musculoskeletal issues such as plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy, and golfer's/tennis elbow. By physically breaking through stagnant scar tissue and forcing a new healing cycle, we provide a pathway to recovery for patients who have failed traditional therapy models.
