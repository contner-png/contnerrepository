Red Light Therapy & Mitochondrial Support

A non-invasive clinical modality utilizing specific wavelengths of light to trigger cellular ATP production and reduce systemic inflammation.

The Science

Red light and near-infrared light penetrate deep into the tissues to reach the mitochondria—the powerhouse of your cells. This light energy is absorbed by cytochrome c oxidase, increasing the production of Adenosine Triphosphate (ATP) and reducing oxidative stress.

Why We Use It

We utilize high-output Red Light Therapy to accelerate recovery from chronic injuries and to support chronic fatigue and long COVID recovery protocols. It is a critical tool for down-regulating systemic inflammation and improving cellular efficiency.
