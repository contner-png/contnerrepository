Mechanism of Action

Every cell in your body maintains a transmembrane potential (voltage). Degraded voltage is the primary marker of inflammation and cellular dysfunction. PEMF therapy induces a micro-current within the tissue, actively resetting this transmembrane potential to its optimal range. By restoring the electrical charge of the cellular membrane, PEMF optimizes ion exchange (enhanced calcium/sodium flow), accelerating cellular tissue repair and down-regulating nociceptive pain signaling.
