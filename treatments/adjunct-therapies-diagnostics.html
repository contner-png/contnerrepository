Supporting Technology

Adjunct Therapies & Diagnostics

Our evidence-based model is supported by a comprehensive suite of diagnostic tools and therapeutic modalities designed to optimize physiological signal and structural integrity.

Spinal Decompression

A non-surgical, computerized traction therapy designed to relieve pressure on the spinal discs and nerves. By creating negative intradiscal pressure, we facilitate the retraction of herniated or bulging disc material and stimulate the flow of nutrient-rich fluids back into the disc space.

Targeted Supplements

We utilize pharmaceutical-grade nutraceuticals to support mitochondrial function, reduce systemic inflammation, and optimize hormonal pathways. Every supplementation strategy is based on objective biomarker data.

Longevity Blood Panels

Our comprehensive clinical diagnostics go beyond standard lab work. We analyze advanced markers of metabolic health, cardiovascular risk, and biological age to engineering your personalized recovery blueprint.

Clinical Sleep Testing

Objective analysis of sleep architecture and autonomic regulation. We identify the physiological roadblocks preventing deep-wave rest and cognitive recovery.

tVNS Neuro-Modulation

Transcutaneous Vagus Nerve Stimulation (tVNS) allows us to manually shift the body out of chronic sympathetic stress. By stimulating the vagus nerve, we facilitate restorative recovery and systemic neuro-inflammation reduction.
