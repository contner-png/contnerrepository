Longevity Diagnostics

Gold-Standard VO2 Max Testing

Mechanism of Action

This assessment is the definitive metric for measuring cardiorespiratory healthspan and athletic endurance. By measuring maximal oxygen uptake during a graded exercise test, we are objectively analyzing the functional aerobic capacity of your pulmonary, cardiovascular, and muscular systems. It provides the gold-standard baseline for engineering personalized metabolic longevity protocols.

What Gets Measured Gets Optimized

VO2 Max is one of the single strongest predictors of all-cause mortality. By objectively identifying your current aerobic ceiling, our clinical team can design specific training and recovery strategies to improve your cardiovascular efficiency and expand your functional longevity.
