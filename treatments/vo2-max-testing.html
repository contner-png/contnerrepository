VO2 Max Testing

The absolute clinical gold standard assessment for measuring your ultimate cardiorespiratory fitness, aerobic healthspan, and metabolic efficiency.

Mechanism of Action

The Science

Through a graded exercise test utilizing a clinical metabolic cart, we precisely measure the maximum volume of oxygen your body can transport and utilize at peak exertion. It provides an exact, objective metric of your mitochondrial efficiency and cardiovascular capacity.

Clinical Application

Why We Use It

A high VO2 Max is widely recognized in scientific literature as one of the strongest predictors of longevity and delayed all-cause mortality. It gives our team the baseline data required to design your personalized cardiometabolic repair protocols.
