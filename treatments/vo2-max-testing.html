Clinical Integrated Modality

VO2 Max Testing

The absolute clinical gold standard assessment for measuring your ultimate cardiorespiratory fitness, aerobic healthspan, and metabolic efficiency.

Mechanism of Action

The Science

Utilizing the clinical-grade PNOE metabolic cart, we perform a graded respiratory assessment to measure oxygen kinetics. While peak testing is available, we primarily utilize submaximal protocols for most patients—providing highly accurate results without requiring maximum physical stress. This allows us to map your mitochondrial efficiency, fat-burning crossover points, and cellular respiration with medical precision.

Clinical Application

Why We Use It

A high VO2 Max is widely recognized in scientific literature as one of the strongest predictors of longevity and delayed all-cause mortality. It gives our team the baseline data required to design your personalized cardiometabolic repair protocols.

Metabolic Data Stack

The PNOE Result Matrix

The PNOE clinical metabolic cart provides over 23 biological markers. Our analysis distills these into a clear, actionable roadmap for your healthspan.

Aerobic Capacity (VO2 Max)

The #1 objective predictor of all-cause mortality. We measure your oxygen ceiling to determine your true biological durability.

Metabolic Efficiency

Precisely identify your Fat-Burning vs. Carbohydrate-Burning crossover points to optimize weight management and energy.

Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR)

Understand exactly how many calories your body burns at rest, removing the guesswork from your nutritional planning.

Lung Strength & Function

Measurement of breathing economy and respiratory stability to ensure your lungs are not a bottleneck for performance.

Biological Age

An objective assessment of your cardiorespiratory fitness relative to your chronological age—the ultimate longevity marker.
