Clinical Integrated Modality

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT)

A pressurized, relaxing environment that safely increases systemic oxygen delivery to your body's tissues and central nervous system.

Mechanism of Action

The Science

Under normal conditions, oxygen delivery is limited by your red blood cells. Inside our hyperbaric chamber, increased atmospheric pressure allows oxygen to dissolve directly into your blood plasma and cerebrospinal fluid. This hyper-oxygenated state is clinically shown to reduce tissue hypoxia (lack of oxygen) and down-regulate inflammatory markers.

Clinical Application

Why We Use It

While widely recognized for specialized wound healing, a growing body of emerging research supports its use for systemic recovery. We utilize HBOT to promote an optimal healing environment for patients managing deep, systemic fatigue, neuro-inflammation, and post-viral recovery.
