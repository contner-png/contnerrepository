What to expect on your first visit

Your initial clinical consultation is a comprehensive 60-minute evaluation designed to establish your physiological baseline. We move beyond standard symptom checklists to identify the root cause of your dysfunction.

  • 1

    Comprehensive Intake: Deep-dive into your clinical history, lifestyle, and performance goals.

  • 2

    Structural & Neurological Scan: Precision diagnostic testing of joint kinematics and nervous system function.

  • 3

    Treatment Blueprint: We engineering a customized recovery and longevity protocol based on your unique data.
