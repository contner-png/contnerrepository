Our Different Treatment Strategy

Most "golf fitness" programs focus on general stretching. We focus on Structural & Biomechanical Durability under rotational load. A powerful golf swing requires both extreme mobility in the thoracic spine and hips, and extreme stability in the lumbar spine.

Our strategy utilizes a Kinematic Sequence Analysis. By identifying the specific joint fixations that prevent you from reaching an optimal "X-Factor" (the differential between shoulder and hip rotation), we allow for a more efficient transfer of energy. We then reinforce this mobility with rotational core stability training to ensure that your physical chassis can withstand the massive torque generated by the modern swing without developing chronic spinal degeneration.
