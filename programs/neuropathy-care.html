Our Different Treatment Strategy

We approach neuropathy as a biological supply chain failure. When the micro-capillaries supplying blood to the nerves begin to die (ischemia), the nerves begin to signal pain and eventually lose function.

Our strategy is built upon Applied Regenerative Physics. By utilizing specific wavelengths of light (Class IV Laser) and acoustic pressure waves (Shockwave), we physically force the production of Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor (VEGF). This triggers angiogenesis—the creation of new blood vessels—to restore the supply chain and allow the peripheral nerves to recover and regenerate.
